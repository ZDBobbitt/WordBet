How to Play

The Grid

A 4×4 grid of random letters appears each round. Each letter carries a point value shown in the corner of the tile.

Forming Words

Click and drag through adjacent tiles to spell a word. Words must be at least 4 letters and valid English words.

The Bet

You have 60 seconds to find the highest-scoring word you can. When time is up, the game secretly finds every valid word on the grid.

Then you're asked: does your best word beat 80% of all possible words? Bet between 1 and 3 gemstones based on your confidence.

If you bet incorrectly, you lose that number of gemstones. If you bet correctly, you win that number of gemstones.

How to Win

You start with 10 gemstones. If you reach 20 gemstones, you win. If you reach 0 gemstones, you lose.
